ListlyAI Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

ListlyAI helps users create meal ideas and grocery lists. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information the app uses

ListlyAI may use meal planning prompts, preferences, selected meals, grocery list items, household profiles, and saved lists that you enter or create in the app.

ListlyAI may use approximate device location only if you grant location permission, to help suggest nearby stores and regional grocery price estimates.

ListlyAI uses subscription status from Apple StoreKit to unlock ListlyAI Pro features.

How information is used

Meal planning prompts and related preferences may be sent to ListlyAI's server-side AI service to generate meal ideas, recipes, and grocery lists. The OpenAI API key is stored only on the backend server and is not included in the iOS app.

Saved meals, lists, folders, household profiles, and usage counters are stored on your device. Some saved app content may sync through Apple's iCloud key-value storage if iCloud is enabled on your device.

Location is used only for location-aware grocery planning features. ListlyAI does not use location for tracking.

Information sharing

ListlyAI does not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers needed to operate the app, including Apple for subscriptions and the backend AI provider for meal and grocery generation.

Analytics and advertising

ListlyAI does not include third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

Data retention

Device-local saved content remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. AI requests may be processed transiently by the backend service and AI provider to return results.
